\begin{abstract}
\noindent A condensed but self-contained reference for single-variable calculus:
limits and continuity, the derivative and its rules, applications of the derivative
(monotonicity, optimization, related rates, linearization), the Riemann integral,
the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus, techniques of integration, and applications
of the integral. Each section pairs a tight statement of the theory with at least
one worked example and a short list of common pitfalls.
\end{abstract}
