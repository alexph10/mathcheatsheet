\begin{abstract}
\noindent A working reference for the standard second-semester calculus material:
sequences and their limits; infinite series and the full battery of convergence
tests; power series, radius of convergence, and Taylor / Maclaurin expansions;
parametric and polar curves with their calculus (slopes, arc length, area);
and an introduction to first-order ordinary differential equations.
Each section pairs definitions and theorems with at least one worked example
and a short list of common pitfalls.
\end{abstract}
