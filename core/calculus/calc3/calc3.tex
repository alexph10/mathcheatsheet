\begin{abstract}
\noindent A working reference for multivariable calculus: vectors and the
geometry of $\R^3$; vector-valued functions, arc length, curvature;
functions of several variables (limits, partials, chain rule, gradients,
directional derivatives, tangent planes); extrema and Lagrange multipliers;
double and triple integrals in Cartesian, polar, cylindrical, and spherical
coordinates; vector fields and line integrals; and the three theorems of
vector calculus --- Green, Stokes, and Divergence.
\end{abstract}
